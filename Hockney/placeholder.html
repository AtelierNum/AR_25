Hockney

Hockney is a British painter, draftsman, printmaker, and architectural designer. He is known for his contributions to the pop art movement of the 1960s and his innovative use of technology in art.

Hockney's work often features bright colors and bold compositions, and he is known for his depictions of landscapes, portraits, and still lifes. He has also experimented with photography and digital art, using iPads and iPhones to create new forms of artistic expression.
